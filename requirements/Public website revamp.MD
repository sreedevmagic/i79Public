# 1. Objective

Reposition i79.ai as a **product-led AI hiring platform**:

# 2. Strategic Principles (MANDATORY)

## P1: Product First
- i79 Engage must be the first visible and dominant element
- Homepage must drive trial/demo

## P2: Single Entry Point
- Focus messaging on:
  👉 AI Interview & Screening Automation

## P3: Layered Product Discovery
- Entry → AI Interviews  
- Expansion → Full AI Hiring OS

## P4: Services as Secondary
- Services support credibility
- Services must NOT compete with product

## P5: Transparent Pricing
- Pricing must be visible
- Enterprise can remain “Contact Sales”
